About the College

Loyola College-Mettala

Loyola College of Arts and Science affiliated to Periyar University, Salem is situated at Loyola Nagar, Mettala near Oilpatty in Nammakal District. "Faith that does justice" has been the hall mark of the Jesuits and we Jesuits try to achieve this through education which empowers the powerless. Loyola College of Arts & Science was established in 2015 by the Jesuits, as envisioned by the Bishop of Salem Rev. Dr. Singaroyan and the clergy, who proposed to start a Catholic College to make higher education available for the weaker and underprivileged sections of the Society.


Department of

Computer Science

The department of Computer Science is one of the leading department in the college. The objective of the department is to enrich computer knowledge of men and women from the rural background. It was started in 2015 with 10 students and two staff. It is one of the popular courses for the students who dream of building their career in the IT (Information Technology) field. The Department has Well Equipped Computer Lab and Spacious Classrooms which enable a healthy Teaching Learning process. ICT (Information and Communication Technology) enabled teaching and learning process help out the students to learn easily and observed quickly. We are conducting Guest Lectures, Seminars, Workshops, Exhibition, Video Conference programs for students to increase the knowledge.
